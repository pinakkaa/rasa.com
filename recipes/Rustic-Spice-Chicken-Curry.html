RASA – Rustic Spice
Chicken Curry

Bold, comforting, and effortlessly rich. A slow-simmered curry with golden onions, ripe tomatoes, and the deep warmth of coconut milk — brought together by the RASA Rustic Spice blend. No fuss, just flavour.

Prep Time 15 min
Cook Time 40–55 min
Serves 4–6 people
Difficulty Simple

The Method

5 Simple Steps

1
Sauté the onions

Heat 1 tbsp oil in a heavy pan. Add sliced onions and sauté for 5 minutes until soft and lightly golden.

2
Build the base

Add chopped tomatoes and sauté for another 5 minutes until they soften and blend into the onions.

3
Add the paste

Stir in 1 tbsp ginger–garlic paste and sauté for 5 minutes until the raw aroma cooks out completely.

4
Add RASA & chicken

Add RASA Rustic Spice powder, salt, and chicken. Sauté for 5 minutes until the masala coats every piece evenly.

1 kg chicken
5 tbsp RASA + 1 tsp salt
500 g chicken
2½ tbsp RASA + ½ tsp salt
5
Simmer low & slow

Cover with a tight lid, lower the heat, and cook until the chicken is completely tender. Stir occasionally to evenly distribute the cooking. Ten minutes before turning off the flame, stir in 50–100 ml coconut milk to taste.

No babysitting needed — go enjoy a cup of tea or a chat with family and friends.

⏱ 500g — approx. 40 mins ⏱ 1 kg — approx. 55 mins
From the Kitchen

Chef's Tip

👨‍🍳
Pro Tip

"When oil rises to the top, your curry is ready."

That pool of richly spiced oil floating on the surface is your signal — the water has cooked off and the masala has fully bloomed. This is the moment the flavours are at their peak. Add your coconut milk just before this point for a silky, well-balanced finish.
