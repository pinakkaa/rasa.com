The Method

5 Simple Steps

1
Build the base

Heat 1 tbsp oil in a heavy pan. Add 2 sliced onions, sauté for 5 mins until golden. Add 2 chopped tomatoes, cook for another 5 mins.

Stir in 1.5 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, cook 5 mins, then fold in a generous handful of chopped mint and coriander leaves.

2
Add RASA & chicken

Add RASA Earthen Spice powder, salt, and chicken. Sauté for 5 minutes until the masala coats every piece evenly.

1 kg chicken
3 tbsp RASA + 1 tsp salt
500 g chicken
1.5 tbsp RASA + ½ tsp salt
3
Simmer low & slow

Cover with a tight lid and simmer on low heat until the chicken is completely tender and cooked through.

⏱ 500g — approx. 40 mins ⏱ 1 kg — approx. 55 mins
4
Cook the rice separately

Boil rice in salted water until just cooked, then drain completely before layering.

1 kg chicken
2½ cups rice · 2 tsp salt · 7 cups water
500 g chicken
1 cup rice · ¾ tsp salt · 3 cups water
5
Layer, close & garnish

Add cooked rice to the chicken pot. Close the lid and gently fold — do not stir vigorously or the rice will break.

Finish with fried onions, boiled eggs, and fresh coriander. Serve straight from the pot.

Pro Tip

"Garnish with fried onions, boiled eggs, and chopped coriander for a truly festive touch."

Quick Fried Onions (Birista) Sauté 3 thinly sliced onions in 2 tbsp oil over low heat for 15–20 minutes until soft and lightly golden. Add 2 tbsp sugar or jaggery with a pinch of salt. Toss in cashews and fry together for 5 more minutes until everything is deep golden and fragrant. These keep well and transform every biryani.
